Cancellation And Refund Policy Services

All bookings are for a minimum of one month period. Booking for the current month cannot be cancelled and no refunds would be made if one wishes to discontinue the service in the middle of the month. Future booking for the following month can be cancelled before the 26th of the preceding month. Your membership commences immediately after your credit card is charged or bank account debited or cheque is realized or cash is received by us. Service purchases will appear on your credit card and/or bank statements under the name.

Credit Card Processing Services

All membership service fees/charges are in Indian currency or as otherwise stated and therefore will accompany a Credit Card Transaction Fee of 1% (depending on your financial institution). Mer Bus Services Pvt. Ltd. does not cover or issue refunds for Credit Card Transaction Fees.
